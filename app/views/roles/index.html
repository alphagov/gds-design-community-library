The Design community and roles at GDS

Design community

There are approximate 65 of us here in the GDS Design Community, and our communuity is still growing. We have graphic, interaction and service designers in GDS. We work across the following areas:

  • Digital Identity
  • GOV.UK
  • Products and Services
  • Service transformation
  • i.AI

Communicating as a community

Slack is the primary way that we communicate as a community. We have a number of channels that are helpful for you be a part of, as they will keep you updates on what is happening.

  • #design
  • #graphicdesigners
  • #interaction-designers
  • #service-designers
  • #design-assessment
  • #govuk-design-system

There is also a cross-government Slack that you can be invited to join. This has users from across government who can ask questions, provide advice or even just chat. There are some design specific channels that are recommended that you join, if you choose to be a part of the cross-government Slack:

  • #design
  • #prototype-kit
  • #servicedesign
  • #design-ethics
  • #climate-emergency

Mailing lists

You can email all designers at GDS at gds-designers@digital.cabinet-office.gov.uk (opens in email client).

Design Community Meetings

We meet every alternate Thursday at 2pm using Google Meet. We also will meet for community away days once a quarter. These are away days are organised by the Design Leads.
