Design Roles at GDS

Design community

Our community is made up of a mix of people from a variety of backgrounds. We have folks who have always known that they were going to work in design, as well as others who have worked in very different professions. This vast wealth of experience means that our design community can draw on our experiences, as well as others to when designing.

The design community shares best practice, supports each other to grow professionally and work together to design good services for the government. We draw on each other's energy and design expertise.
