Laura Yarrow

Head of Design for GOV.UK

Role at GDS

Laura is the Head of Design for GOV.UK. This is a massive undertaking and covers the Publishing teams, Insights and Analytics, Accessibility Monitoring team, Branding and Social team, Content operation, Policy and Strategy team and GOV.UK Design System team.

Laura is keen to ensure that we have a cross cutting channel approach to GOV.UK, tapping into the social aspect that we've been missing. She has been advocating for a stronger brand presence, and working out where we can build our presence across users lives. She has also been developing strong relationships with other Heads of Design across government, building on our reputation and listening to what we are missing.

Biography

Over the past five years in government, Laura has led design at both GDS and HM Land Registry, applying her expertise in user-centred design to deliver accessible, effective services across the public sector.

Her career spans more than two decades in the digital industry spanning web development, user research and design, providing her with deep experience in developing intuitive products that meet real human needs.

What drives her work is a curiosity about people: how they think, what they need, and why they behave the way they do. This people-first approach underpins her commitment to creating experiences that are simple, accessible, inclusive, and genuinely useful.
