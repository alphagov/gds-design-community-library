Role at GDS

Helena is head of UCD for Digital Identity. She works within the central UCD pod, casting an eye over everything that comes into the DI directorate and working our where strings of work can be joined up. She provides advice on how to think about things from both design and accessibility perspectives, and always champions the work of her teams.

Helena is someone that has extensive knowledge of the OneLogin service, and if she doesn't have the answer you need, she will know the person who will. She's constantly evolving the way in which designers in her directorate work, to ensure that they are streamlined and not duplicating effort.
