Colin Burns

Chief Design Officer (Interim)

picture of person here

Role at GDS

Colin is here to put Design at the head of the table. Working at the same level as other Directors, he's here to enable and facilate difficult conversations about when design needs to be involved, as well as encourage early adoption of knowledge and experience.

He has also worked alongside the Heads of Design to develop pathways for career progression, including the introduction of a new role; the design delivery manager. This new role will open up a new area of design and bridge the gap between design and delivery.

Biography

Colin is a creative leader and design manager, with over 30 years working in multi-disciplinary teams.

With a training in Industrial Design and working in Interaction Design, he has been part of a number of storied creative organisations across a wide range of industries, including Technology, Media, FMCG and Retail.

His perspectives on making the most of the resources that organisations have for innovation have been shaped by working with organisations like, Apple, BBC, IDEO, British Airways, Prada, ASDA, Procter & Gamble, Unilever and Hewlett Packard.
